Frequently Asked Questions

1. General Questions

Q1: What is CarPal?

CarPal is an innovative web platform designed to simplify operations for auto repair shops. It offers tools for invoice generation, customer management, payment tracking, and analytics, all within an easy-to-use interface.

Q2: How do I create an account?

1. Visit the CarPal registration page.
2. Fill in your shop's details, including your business name, contact information, and payment details.
3. Confirm your email and phone number through the provided verification codes.

Q3: Can I use CarPal on multiple devices?

Yes, CarPal is accessible on any device with a web browser, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones.

2. Account Management

Q4: How do I reset my password?

1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Follow the password reset instructions sent to your email.

Q5: How do I update my business information?

1. Navigate to the Settings page in your account and update your business details under the "Business Profile" section.

Q6: What happens if my account is inactive for a long time?

1. Inactive accounts are not deleted but may be logged out automatically for security reasons. You can log back in at any time to resume using the platform.

3. Invoices and Payments

Q7: How do I generate an invoice?

1. Go to the "Invoices" page.
2. Click on "Create New Invoice."
3. Add service items, parts, and other details.
4. Preview and save or send the invoice to your customer.

Q8: Can customers pay directly through CarPal?

Yes, CarPal integrates with M-Pesa Till numbers for secure payment verification.

Q9: How do I track overdue payments?

The "Invoices" page has a dedicated section for tracking overdue payments. Notifications will also alert you to overdue invoices.

4. Customer Management

Q10: How do I add a new customer?

1. Navigate to the "Customers" page.
2. Click on "Add New Customer."
3. Enter the customer's details and save.

Q11: Can I see a customer's service history?

Yes, each customer's profile contains their complete service and payment history.

Q12: Can I send service reminders to customers?

Yes, CarPal allows you to send automated reminders for upcoming services, pending invoices, or promotions.

5. Reports and Analytics

Q13: What types of reports can I generate?

CarPal provides reports on earnings, payment trends, outstanding invoices, and customer activity.

Q14: Can I export reports?

Yes, reports can be exported in Excel, CSV, or PDF formats.

Q15: How often are analytics updated?

Analytics are updated in real time to reflect your shop's most recent activity.

6. Notifications

Q16: What types of notifications will I receive?

You will receive notifications for new invoices, payment confirmations, overdue payments, service updates, and more.

Q17: How do I manage notification preferences?

Notification preferences can be adjusted in the "Settings" section under "Notifications."

8. Security and Privacy

Q20: How is my data protected?

CarPal employs advanced encryption and complies with Kenya's Data Protection Act, 2019, to ensure your data is secure.

Q21: Can I delete my account and data?

Yes, you can request account and data deletion by contacting legal@citruslabs.co.ke.

9. Pricing and Fees

Q22: Is there a subscription fee for CarPal?

Yes, CarPal operates on a subscription model. Details about pricing are available on the pricing page of the platform.

Q23: Are there additional charges?

CarPal charges a 10% service fee for payments processed through the platform.

10. Integrations and Features

Q24: Can I integrate CarPal with other systems?

Currently, CarPal supports M-Pesa integration for payments. Additional integrations are planned for future updates.

Q25: Does CarPal support dark mode?

Yes, you can enable dark mode from the footer of the platform or in the "Settings" section.
