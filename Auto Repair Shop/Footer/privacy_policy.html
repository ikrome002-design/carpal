Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1st January 2025

Contact Us:

  • Email: legal@citruslabs.co.ke

1. Scope of the Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the CarPal platform, including but not limited to:

  • Auto Repair Shop users
  • Sales Personnel users
  • Administrator account users
  • End customers engaging with the platform through business accounts

2. Data We Collect

a) Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you provide to us when you:

  • Register for an account
  • Submit inquiries, forms, or support requests
  • Conduct transactions through the platform
  • Participate in surveys or promotional activities

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data to:

  1. Facilitate account creation and manage user authentication
  2. Enable transactions between users, such as between Sales Personnel and Auto Repair Shops
  3. Process payments, calculate service fees, and issue invoices
  4. Deliver platform notifications, such as service updates or policy changes
  5. Provide customer support and address user inquiries
  6. Improve and optimize the platform through analytics
  7. Ensure compliance with legal and regulatory requirements
  8. Conduct marketing, subject to obtaining your explicit consent where required

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it under the following circumstances:

a) With Your Consent

We may share your data with third parties if you provide explicit consent.

b) With Service Providers

We may share data with trusted service providers to:

  • Process payments (e.g., M-Pesa integrations)
  • Host and maintain our platform
  • Provide customer support or marketing services

c) For Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information to:

  • Comply with a legal obligation, court order, or government request
  • Protect the rights, property, or safety of Citrus Labs, our users, or the public

d) In Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the new entity.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. Retention periods may be extended where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as resolving disputes or maintaining security.

6. Your Rights

Under Kenyan law, you have the following rights:

  1. Access: You may request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  2. Correction: You may ask us to correct any inaccurate or incomplete data
  3. Erasure: You may request that we delete your data, subject to legal obligations
  4. Objection: You may object to the processing of your data for certain purposes
  5. Data Portability: You may request a transfer of your data to another entity

To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@citruslabs.co.ke.

7. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Regular audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Restricted access to sensitive information

Despite these measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to protect their account credentials and report any suspicious activity.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Enhance platform functionality
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Deliver personalized content and advertisements

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices, and we encourage you to review their privacy policies.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted on the platform with a revised effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: legal@citruslabs.co.ke
