CarPal Platform Data Policy

Contact Information

  • Email: legal@citruslabs.co.ke

Effective Date: 1st January 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the CarPal platform. This Data Policy outlines how Citrus Labs Ltd., the owner of the CarPal platform ("we," "our," "us"), collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data in compliance with the Data Protection Act, 2019 (Kenya) and other relevant regulations as of 2025.

By using the CarPal platform, you ("user," "you") agree to the terms outlined in this Data Policy. This Policy applies to all users of the platform, including auto repair shop owners, sales personnel, administrators, and customers accessing services.

2. Definitions

  • "Personal Data": Any information relating to an identifiable individual, such as name, contact details, financial information, or IP address.
  • "Data Subject": An individual whose personal data is being processed.
  • "Processing": Any operation performed on personal data, including collection, storage, use, disclosure, or deletion.
  • "Platform": The CarPal web application and associated services.

3. Data We Collect

We may collect the following categories of data:

3.1 Personal Information

  • Name, contact details (phone number, email address)
  • Identification documents (e.g., business registration certificates for auto repair shops)

3.2 Financial Information

  • Bank account details and M-Pesa numbers for transactions
  • Payment history and invoice records

3.3 Technical Information

  • IP addresses, device identifiers, browser types
  • Cookies and usage logs for analytics and performance monitoring

3.4 Other Information

  • Feedback, queries, and correspondence with our customer support team

4. How We Collect Data

Data may be collected:

  • Directly from users: During account creation, service inquiries, or transactions
  • Automatically: Through cookies, web beacons, and usage tracking on the platform
  • From third parties: Referral partners, payment processors, or publicly available sources

5. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect and process data for the following purposes:

  • Account Management: To create, verify, and manage user accounts
  • Service Provision: To facilitate transactions, invoicing, and service management
  • Communication: To respond to inquiries, send updates, and provide support
  • Analytics and Improvement: To monitor usage trends and enhance platform functionality
  • Compliance: To meet legal obligations, including anti-fraud and tax regulations

6. Legal Basis for Data Processing

We process personal data based on:

  • Consent: Obtained when users register, provide information, or accept cookies
  • Contractual Obligations: For providing services agreed upon in our terms
  • Legal Compliance: To adhere to laws such as the Data Protection Act, 2019
  • Legitimate Interests: For fraud prevention, security, and service improvement

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell personal data. However, we may share data with:

  • Service Providers: To facilitate payments, analytics, and IT support
  • Law Enforcement: When required by law or court order
  • Affiliated Entities: For internal business processes or platform integration
  • Business Transfers: In case of mergers, acquisitions, or sales of the business

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for:

  • Providing services
  • Meeting legal or regulatory requirements
  • Resolving disputes or enforcing agreements

Data no longer required is securely deleted or anonymized.

9. User Rights

Under Kenyan law, users have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of personal data held about them
  • Rectify: Correct inaccuracies in their data
  • Delete: Request data erasure (subject to legal or contractual obligations)
  • Restrict Processing: Limit data usage under certain conditions
  • Data Portability: Obtain a copy of their data in a portable format
  • Withdraw Consent: Revoke previously given consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at legal@citruslabs.co.ke or call +254 712 400 000.

10. Data Security

We employ robust security measures to protect personal data:

  • Encryption: For data transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: To limit data access to authorized personnel only
  • Regular Audits: To assess and enhance security protocols
  • Incident Response Plan: To address data breaches promptly

11. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze platform performance. Users can:

  • Accept or decline cookies via browser settings
  • Access detailed cookie preferences on the platform

12. Data Transfers

Where data is transferred outside Kenya, we ensure:

  • Compliance with cross-border data transfer regulations
  • Adequate data protection standards in recipient jurisdictions

13. Complaints

If you believe your data rights have been violated, you may:

  • Contact us directly at legal@citruslabs.co.ke
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) in Kenya

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Data Policy periodically. Users will be notified of significant changes via email or platform announcements. Continued use of the platform after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

15. Additional Information

For inquiries, feedback, or further details, contact:
