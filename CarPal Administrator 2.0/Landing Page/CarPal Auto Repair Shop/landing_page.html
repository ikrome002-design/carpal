About CarPal

Platform Overview

CarPal is a cutting-edge SaaS platform designed specifically for auto repair shops. We streamline every aspect of the service process—from vehicle check-in to final invoicing.

Our Mission

To empower auto repair shops with a connected, efficient, and user-centric platform that transforms everyday challenges into opportunities for growth and success.
