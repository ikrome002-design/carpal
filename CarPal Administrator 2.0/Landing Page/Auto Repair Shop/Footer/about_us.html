About Us: CarPal Platform for Auto Repair Shops

Welcome to CarPal, your trusted partner in revolutionizing the auto repair industry. We're an innovative web platform designed to streamline operations for auto repair shops, enhance customer experiences, and empower businesses to grow in the ever-evolving automotive sector.

Our Mission

At CarPal, our mission is to simplify and modernize the way auto repair shops operate by providing a user-friendly platform that integrates essential tools and resources. We aim to foster efficiency, transparency, and professionalism in the automotive repair ecosystem, making it easier for businesses to thrive.

Who We Are

CarPal is developed and managed by Citrus Labs Ltd., a Kenyan-based technology company committed to delivering cutting-edge solutions for small and medium-sized enterprises. Headquartered in the heart of Nairobi at Britam Towers, our team combines technical expertise with a deep understanding of the local automotive industry to bring you a platform tailored to your needs.

What We Offer

Streamlined Account Management

Create and manage your auto repair shop profile effortlessly. Track business details, services, and customer interactions in one place.

Invoicing and Payment Tracking

Generate professional invoices with ease. Keep track of pending, completed, and overdue payments. Integrate with Mpesa Till Numbers for secure payment verification.

Customer Relationship Management

Maintain a detailed database of your customers. Access service histories and payment records for better customer service.
