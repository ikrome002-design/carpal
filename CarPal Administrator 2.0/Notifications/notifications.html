System Maintenance Scheduled

Today, 10:45 AM High Priority

Notification Content

Dear Platform Administrator,

This is to inform you that system maintenance is scheduled for March 30, 2025, from 2:00 AM to 4:00 AM UTC. The platform may experience brief downtime during this period.

During the maintenance window, the following services will be affected:

  • User registration and authentication
  • Payment processing
  • Real-time tracking

The maintenance is necessary to implement critical security patches and performance improvements. We recommend informing business users about this planned downtime.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the technical support team.
